    About Us

    The Student Magazine is a student-led digital publication created to share relevant information, opportunities, and stories from across the university. The platform brings together articles covering academic support, campus life, events, and student opportunities, making it easier for students to find information in one place.

    The magazine is designed to be simple to use and easy to navigate, with content presented in a clear, editorial-style layout inspired by traditional print magazines. The focus is on readability, accessibility, and helping students quickly access articles that are useful to them. Alongside browsing articles, users can choose to save content or subscribe to the newsletter to stay up to date.

    The platform aims to support students throughout their time at university by providing practical, relevant, and engaging content in a format that feels familiar and approachable.
